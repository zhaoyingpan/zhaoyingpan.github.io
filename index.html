Current Research

Motion Magnification
Advisor: Prof. Andrew Owens
  • I implemented an approach for motion magnification with a sinusoidal representation network and a single video example as a toy example to demonstrate the effectiveness of using optical flow to guide the generation of magnified frames.
  • I am currently working on training a network on unlabeled video data for motion magnification with a large range of flexible magnification factors.
  • The GIF shows the results with magnification factor of 2/4/8.
Prompt Analysis for Diffusion Models (under review)
Collaborator: Jinge Ma and Yutong Xie. Advisor: Prof. Qiaozhu Mei
  • We explored the large-scale prompt logs collected from multiple text-to-image generation systems to investigate the information needs.
  • We compared the prompt logs to the query log of web search engines, and concluded the patterns of prompt logs.
  • We provided concrete implications on how to improve text-to-image generation systems for creation purposes.
  • The image shows the UMAP visualization of prompts from different datasets, which indicates gaps between prompts in the training dataset and the user-input prompts.

Projects

Bachelor's Thesis: Image Caption Generating of High-Resolution Remote Sensing Images Nov. 2020 - Apr. 2021
Advisor: Prof. Kun Fu and Prof. Xian Sun
Check my thesis (Chinese version) here!
  • I implemented image captioning algorithms, including Show and Tell, Show, Attend and Tell, Transformer, Attention on Attention (AoA), on three remote-sensing image datasets, Sydney-Captions Dataset, UCM-Captions Dataset, and RSICD Dataset.
  • I compared and analyzed the trained models qualitatively and quantitatively to determine the best model for practical application.
  • The conclusion is that the Show, Attend and Tell and Transformer trained on RSICD dataset are recommended for applications.
  • The image shows an example of captions for remote sensing images, from RSICD dataset.
Object Detection Implementation for Remote Sensing ImagesAug. 2020 - Oct. 2020
Advisor: Prof. Kun Fu and Prof. Xian Sun
  • I reviewed object detection algorithms, including Faster-RCNN, YOLO v3, and YOLO v4.
